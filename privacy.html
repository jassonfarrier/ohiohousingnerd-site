Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Jasson Farrier, Realtor · Kiger Realty

Scope

We believe that you should know what information we collect from you and how we use it, and that you should have meaningful control over both. This Notice describes how we collect and use information, including personal information, with whom we share information, your rights and choices, and how you can contact us about our privacy practices.

Please note that Jasson Farrier is an independent real estate agent with Kiger Realty. Kiger Realty is a real estate brokerage company. This Notice does not apply to the privacy practices of the Kiger Realty brokerage office or other independent agents.

Information We Collect

When you access our website, contact us, or otherwise interact with us, we collect personal information about you. Personal information is information about an individual from which that person can be identified. It does not include information where the identity has been removed (anonymous data).

When you contact us: When you contact us with questions about our services, our business, or other matters, we will use any personal information you provided, including your name, email, and phone, to resolve your request or answer your questions. We may place you on our marketing list for information related to your inquiry. You will always have the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from us.

Information we automatically collect: Our website automatically collects certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including details of your visits, traffic data, location data (if enabled), logs, and other communication data. This includes information about your computer, internet connection, IP address, operating system, and browser type.

Technologies we use for automatic data collection include:

  • Cookies: A cookie is a small file placed on your computer. You may refuse browser cookies by adjusting your browser settings, though this may limit access to certain parts of our website.
  • Web Beacons: Pages of our website may contain small electronic files known as web beacons that permit us to count users and collect related website statistics.

How We Use the Information We Collect

  • To fulfill or meet the reason you provided the information
  • To provide, support, personalize, and develop our services
  • To deliver content and offerings relevant to your interests, including via email (with your consent where required by law)
  • To help maintain the safety, security, and integrity of our website and business
  • For research, analysis, and service development
  • To respond to law enforcement requests and as required by applicable law

How We Share the Information We Collect

Service providers: We may share information with service providers who provide services on our behalf such as data hosting or email delivery. We authorize such providers to use information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

Business partners: We may share information with third-party business partners who are contractually committed to protecting the security and confidentiality of your information.

Corporate transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, personal information may be part of the assets transferred, subject to this Notice and your privacy choices.

Legal disclosures: We may share your personal information as required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to prevent fraud, violations of our terms, or illegal activity.

Your Rights and Choices

Email opt-out: If you no longer want to receive marketing emails from us, you may opt out via the unsubscribe link in any email. We will comply with your request promptly. Note that we may still send important administrative messages required to provide our services.

Depending on where you reside, you may also have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at 614-699-3007.

Information Security

We maintain organizational, physical, and technical security measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, destruction, loss, alteration, or misuse. We periodically review and update our security measures. No data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Information Retention

We will only retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Children

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Any personal information you provide to those organizations is governed by their own privacy policies.
