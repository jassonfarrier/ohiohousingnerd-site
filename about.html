Realtor · Kiger Realty Real Estate Investor · Central Ohio Ohio Housing Nerd · YouTube Licking · Franklin · Knox · Delaware Counties

I'm not a perma-doomer. I'm not a permabull. I'm the guy who looks at the actual data and tells you what it says — even when it's not what people want to hear, and even when it contradicts what everyone else is saying.

Every week I go through all 88 Ohio counties. Inventory. New listings. Days on market. Price reductions. Foreclosures. Pending sales. I pull it from Redfin, Realtor.com, Zillow, ATTOM, PropStream, and the Central Ohio MLS. I cross-reference it. I look for what the headlines are missing. Then I put it on YouTube.

"I'm not here to sell you on buying a home. I'm here to make sure you understand what you're walking into."

I've been a Realtor in Ohio working buyers and sellers across Licking, Franklin, Knox, Delaware, Morrow, and Fairfield counties. I invest in real estate myself — buy-and-hold rentals across Central Ohio. So when I analyze this market, I'm not doing it from a distance. I'm in it every day.

A lot of housing analysts online either want the market to crash so they can say they called it, or they're cheerleaders who won't acknowledge any risk. I'm neither. There are people who don't like me because I'm a Realtor and investor — fine. My job is to give you the clearest possible picture of what's actually happening so you can make a smart decision for your own life.

The Ohio Housing Nerd Channel

Ohio Housing Nerd started about a year ago and is growing faster than my national channel despite having less content. That told me something. There's a real gap in the market for serious, data-driven Ohio housing analysis. Not clickbait. Not doom. Not cheerleading. Just the data, broken down clearly, with context that actually makes sense for people living and working in Ohio.

I go live every week and pull up real zip codes, real counties, real numbers — live on camera. Viewers drop their zip code in the comments and I analyze it on the spot. Come watch.

Working With Me as a Realtor
